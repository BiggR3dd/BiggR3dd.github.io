Make My Famous Spicy Pasta!

This recipe is one of my all time favorite meals whenever I have a small party over to the house for the game. It goes great with craft beer, or wine, or whatever really. It's easy and filling, everything you could want for a big game.


Ingredient List

Fresh Garlic
Salt & Pepper
Heavy Cream
Butter
Penne Pasta
1lb Ground Sausage
Red Pepper Flakes
Parmesean Cheese

Step 1
Start by browning the sausage in a large pot, while begining to boil 6 quarts of water

Step 2
Once the sausage is thoroughly cooked through, and your water has begun to boil, add your noodles to the water, and 4tbsp of butter plus 1 cup of heavy cream to the sausage

Step 3
Your noodles should cook for 10 mins, no longer than 12 mins

Step 4
When the cream and butter starts to simmer with the sausage, start adding parmesean cheese a little bit at a time, you will add about 1 cup to 1 and a half cups of cheese while you wait for the noodles to finish

Step 5
Add your seasonings, (Red Pepper Flake, Salt, Pepper), until it tastes right to you

Step 6
As soon as the noodles are done, strain them and then add the noodles to your sauce mixture a little bit at a time, add a little more parmesean cheese, and then serve

All Done! Yummy!!
